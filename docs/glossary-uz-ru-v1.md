## Stop words and prohibited phrasing
- Avoid informal slang (e.g., «чувак», «aka») in both languages.
- Do not promise guaranteed income; use conditional verbs ("может", "mumkin") instead.
- Never translate brand name "Obsidian"; keep Latin spelling.
- Do not use gendered suffixes when addressing users (e.g., "уважаемый"/"уважаемая").
- Replace literal word-for-word translations of financial terms with glossary equivalents (e.g., always use "eskrou hisob" not "depozit").
